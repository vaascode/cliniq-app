## Option A: Story-driven (recommended)

During COVID, I watched my parents stand in line for hours — for vaccination, check-ups, everything.

Elderly people. Pregnant women. Kids. All waiting in crowded clinics with no idea when their turn would come.

That problem didn't go away after COVID. It's still happening every single day in clinics across India.

So I built Cliniq — an app that eliminates the waiting room.

Here's how it works:

**For Patients:**
→ Scan the clinic's QR code or join from home
→ Pick your concern & generate a token instantly
→ Track your position in the queue in real time
→ Get notified when your turn is near
→ Arrive just in time — zero waiting

Book tokens for family members too. Your parents don't need to figure out the app — you can do it for them.

**For Doctors:**
→ See your entire queue live — who's arrived, who hasn't
→ One tap to call the next patient
→ View patient concerns before they walk in
→ Voice notes during consultation
→ Smart analytics — peak hours, top concerns, daily trends

No more shouting names in the hallway. No more "doctor kab aayenge?"

Smarter waiting. Better care.

The app is live. Link in the replies.
